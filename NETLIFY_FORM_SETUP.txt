OriginV6 Netlify Form Setup

Replacement files included:
- property-pathway.html
- assets/js/wizard.js
- index.html and all other site files are included for convenience.

Where submissions go:
1. Deploy this OriginV6 folder to Netlify.
2. In Netlify Dashboard, open your site.
3. Go to Forms.
4. Look for the form named: origin-property-pathway.
5. Submitted leads will appear there.

To receive email notifications:
1. In Netlify Dashboard, go to Forms.
2. Open Form notifications.
3. Add email notification.
4. Send to: hello@originpropertyconcierge.com.au or your preferred email.

Fields captured:
- name
- mobile
- email
- journey
- support
- otherSupportDetails
- pathway JSON with the full questionnaire answers.
